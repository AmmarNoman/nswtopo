About

nswtopo

vector topo maps for NSW, Victoria and Tasmania

nswtopo is open-source software for creating detailed topographic maps of New South Wales, Victoria and Tasmania. Maps can be made in both vector and raster formats.

Get the Code

Code for nswtopo is available on GitHub.

Make maps of any areas you want using the lastest government topographic data. Add extra layers such as shaded relief, grid lines and your own GPS data.

Maps for Mobile

Messing with software isn't for everyone. If you just need some quick topos for your phone or tablet, nswtopo has you covered.

First, grab the free Avenza Maps app for your iOS or Android device.

Then use this site to find the maps you need. Click or tap the maps to purchase them from the App Store or Google Play. Download the maps to your device and you're good to go.

Maps are available for NSW, VIC, TAS, WA and SA at scales of 1:25 000 or 1:50 000 according to area. Each map set is created from official government topographic data for that state.
